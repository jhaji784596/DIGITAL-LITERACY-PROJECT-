# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

* Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
* Do not approve unknown payment requests
* Remember: entering PIN always sends money
* Avoid clicking suspicious payment links
* Verify unknown callers claiming to be bank officials
* Enable app lock on payment applications
* Use official apps only from Play Store/App Store
* Check receiver name before sending money
* Do not scan unknown QR codes for payments
* Report fraud immediately to cybercrime portal

Report Cybercrime:
Website: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
